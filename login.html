About AyurDAO

The Problem

The Ayurvedic industry faces serious challenges — adulteration, lack of transparency and fragmented supply chain records reduce consumer trust and product authenticity.

Our Solution

AyurDAO uses blockchain technology to record every step of the supply chain as an immutable, time-stamped block — ensuring complete transparency from farm to shelf.

The Technology

Built on a custom blockchain with SHA-256 hashing, RESTful APIs, QR code generation and a Digital Twin framework for personalized herb information.
